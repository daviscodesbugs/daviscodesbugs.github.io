## Background

After building a Voron V2, I was hooked. Most of what I print is small — rarely do I use a full build plate — so a compact printer that heats up fast and can rip through parts made a lot of sense. Rigid, quick, purpose-built for the stuff I actually make. It seemed like a fun project, so I jumped in shortly after finishing the V2.

## What it is

A 120mm³ enclosed CoreXY printer running Klipper. Small footprint, fast heat-up, handles ABS and ASA without breaking a sweat. It literally fits inside the V2.

## The Build

Ordered the Formbot kit on Oct 21 and had it built by Nov 5 — about two weeks from box to first print. The kit comes with a BigTreeTech Pi for running Klipper and umbilical PCBs for a clean wiring setup to the toolhead.
