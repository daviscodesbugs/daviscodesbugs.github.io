## Background

I wanted a serious enclosed printer that could handle engineering-grade materials and large parts. The Voron 2.4 is one of the most well-regarded community-designed printers out there — fully enclosed, CoreXY, and built for performance. It's the printer that started the rabbit hole.

## What it is

A 350mm enclosed CoreXY running Klipper. Prints ABS, ASA, PC — anything that benefits from a heated chamber. The Voron Tap probe makes it build-surface independent, and the Stealthburner toolhead keeps things quiet.

## The Build

- **Kit:** LDO Voron 2.4 R2 Rev D (350mm)
- **Toolhead:** Stealthburner with Clockwork2 extruder
- **Hotend:** E3D Revo (Voron)
- **Mainboard:** LDO Leviathan
- **Probe:** Voron Tap
- **Firmware:** Klipper

This build kicked off a whole rabbit hole. After finishing the 2.4, I immediately jumped into building a [[voron-v0|Voron V0.2]] as a compact companion for smaller prints. The V0 literally fits inside the V2's build volume.
