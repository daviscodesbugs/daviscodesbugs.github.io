## What it is

A jump-scare Halloween decoration — a cloaked skeleton figure holding a lantern that lunges forward on a pneumatic piston when triggered wirelessly. One button on the controller, instant scare.

## The Build

- **Skeleton & clothing:** Constructed and dressed by hand
- **Lantern:** Adapted from a plastic model with 3D-printed parts
- **Pneumatics:** Piston and solenoid valve for the lunge motion
- **Electronics:** ESP microcontroller receiving ESPNOW signals to trigger the solenoid
- **Controller:** A separate handheld unit that sends the ESPNOW trigger

ESPNOW is a direct peer-to-peer protocol — no WiFi network needed, so it works reliably outdoors with minimal latency.
